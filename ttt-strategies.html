Pro tip: Don't kill too early. Let the innocents get paranoid of each other first

If you wait until 2-3 people have already died (either from other traitors or RDM), everyone is way more suspicious and it's easier to blend in

Also never walk directly towards your target. Always pretend you're going somewhere else and just "happen" to cross paths
