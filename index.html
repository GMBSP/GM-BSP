TheGMTower is shutting down.

After nearly eight years, we are closing the servers and archiving the forums.

The game servers will go offline on February 1st, 2017. The forums will remain in read-only mode for as long as we can keep the hosting paid.

Thank you. Genuinely. For every surf run, every deathrun attempt, every bug report, every stupid post in General Discussion at 3 AM. It meant something to us.

— Pretzels & the TheGMTower staff team
