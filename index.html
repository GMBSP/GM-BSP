Welcome to The GM Tower

Garry's Mod Community Server - Established 2015

Server Status: OFFLINE

The GM Tower is a Garry's Mod community server featuring multiple gamemodes including Sandbox, Trouble in Terrorist Town (TTT), and Zombie Survival. Join our community and experience the best of GMod!

Available Gamemodes

Sandbox

The classic Garry's Mod experience. Build anything you can imagine with access to all props, tools, and weapons. No rules, no limits - just pure creativity.

  • Unlimited props and tools
  • Physics gun and tool gun
  • Spawn NPCs and vehicles
  • Save and share your creations
  • Workshop content support
Trouble in Terrorist Town

A social deduction gamemode where innocent players must identify and eliminate traitors among them before it's too late.

  • Detective role with special equipment
  • Traitor tools and weapons
  • Custom maps and roles
  • Voice chat for communication
  • Karma system for fair play
Zombie Survival

Survive waves of zombies with other players. Build barricades, work together, and fight for survival against increasingly difficult undead hordes.

  • Wave-based survival gameplay
  • Barricade building system
  • Multiple zombie types
  • Arsenal of weapons
  • Team-based cooperation

How to Connect

To join The GM Tower server:

  1. Launch Garry's Mod
  2. Open the server browser (View > Servers)
  3. Search for "The GM Tower" in the Legacy Browser
  4. Double-click to connect or add to favorites
